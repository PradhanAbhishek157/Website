E-commerce (electronic commerce) is the buying and selling of goods and services, or the transmitting of funds or data using internet as a medium. E-commerce is often used to refer to sale of physical products online, but it can also be described any kind of commercial transaction that is facilited through the internet.


The history of e-commerce begins with the first ever online sale: on August 11, 1994 as a man sold a CD by the band Sting to his friend through his website NetMark, an American retail platform. This was the first example of a consumer purchasing a product from a business through e-commerce as, we commonly know it today. There are many types of e-commerce websites such as Business to Consumer(B2C), Business to Business(B2B), Consumer to Consumer(C2C), Consumer to Business(C2B). Dropshipping, wholesale, retail, crowdfunding, etc. are some examples of e-commerce.


There are various positive effects of e-commerce as organization can easily expand their market to national and internal markets with minimum capital investment, it improves the brand image of the company, it helps organization to provide better customer services, it simplifies the business processes and makes them faster and effecient. Moreover, to this it provides 24*7 support, more option and quicker delivery that makes shopping of products as consumer can shop without visiting the physical store and product reliability to the customers.


Everything has its pros and cons, some of the negative impact of business through e-commerce are consumer personal information privacy threats, security threats if the website doesn't comply with adequate security measures, giving risks to credit card fraud and identity theft, problem in returning the product and various complaints about product quality satisfaction.


Hence, transaction through e-commerce gives benefit to both organitazion and consumer. Therefore, these websites should be used properly to get more out of it as it can be of great use for the society.If the more emphasis is given on making the ecommerce more secure and reliable by reducing the chances of those all negative effects to its minimum then many bussiness as well as consumers can benefit from this in many ways without the compromising their privacy.
